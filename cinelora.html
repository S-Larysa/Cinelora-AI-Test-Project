Questions & Answers

FAQ

Do I need a credit card to sign up?

No. The Free plan requires only an email address and password. You only need payment details when upgrading to Pro or purchasing a credit pack.

What file formats does CINELORA AI support for output?

Images are exported as .png, .jpg or .webp. Videos are exported as .mp4 or .webm. These formats are supported across all major browsers and devices.

How long does generation take?

Image generation typically takes 3–6 seconds. Video generation varies by duration: 4-second clips average around 20–30 seconds, and 12-second clips can take up to a minute. Pro users get priority queue access, so wait times are shorter.

Can I generate multiple variants at once?

Yes! You can generate up to 4 variants from a single prompt. Each variant uses a separate generation credit.

Is my generated content private by default?

Yes. All newly generated content defaults to Private. You can change visibility to Unlisted (shareable by direct link) or Public (appears in the global Explore gallery) at any time from the item detail view.

What happens if I run out of credits mid-generation?

The platform checks your credit balance before accepting a generation request. If you don't have enough credits, you'll be prompted to top up or upgrade your plan. No partial charges are made.
